# CCC 1029

In the glory of heaven the blessed continue joyfully to fulfill God's will in relation to other men and to all creation. Already they reign with Christ; with him "they shall reign for ever and ever."603 596 1 Jn 3:2; cf. 1 Cor 13:12; Rev 22:4. 597 Benedict XII, Benedictus Deus (1336): DS 1000; cf. LG 49. 600 St. Ambrose, In Luc., 10, 121: PL 15, 1834A. 602 St. Cyprian, Ep. 58, 10, 1: CSEL 3/2, 665.
